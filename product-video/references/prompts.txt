PROMPT ENGINE NOTES

- Google video guidance: make subject, action, scene/context, camera angle, and one purposeful camera movement explicit. A locked camera is valid. Describe subtle temporal evolution instead of vague style adjectives. When an engine accepts negative prompts, list unwanted conditions rather than writing command-like “do not” sentences.
- LTX-Video: describe actions and scene chronologically in one flowing paragraph; include literal movement, appearance, camera, and environment; begin with the action and stay under 200 words.
- Wan first-last-frame video: prefer Chinese prompts. Prompt expansion may add detail, but it must not add a second action, unverified function, or new identity feature.
- Feishu reference workflow: establish style/context references and clear product references first, generate scene previews, review and revise prompts, then use the approved images and prompt for video. In this skill, anchor 01 is the scene-preview gate and becomes world_plate.
- Cross-model prompt craft: earlier information carries more weight. Start with the visible change, use one action and one purposeful camera behavior, and remove one demand before adding detail to a failed prompt.
- Reference inputs need jobs. The product source locks identity/material; the previous anchor locks incoming state; the destination anchor locks the exact endpoint; world_plate locks room/light. Do not treat every reference as an undirected style moodboard.

ENGINE PROFILE SELECTION

- `motion_only_i2v` for Veo/Flow/Runway with a strong source frame: the image already defines product, room, light, and composition. Write only the single subject action, one camera behavior, simple environment response, timing, and end state. Avoid re-describing appearance in prose; keep identity/physics as a compact lock.
- `chronological_shot` for LTX/Wan or when the engine needs a fuller description: write one literal chronological paragraph covering the same single action, contact/path/dynamics, camera, environment, and endpoint. Keep LTX under 200 words; keep Wan prompt expansion near 80-100 words and prefer Chinese for first-last-frame generation.
- `editorial_bridge` for an explicitly edited two-beat segment in the same established task: one beat ends, one occlusion/match_action cut occurs, and the second beat lands on the supplied end frame. Use only with a multi-shot-capable engine or NLE; never send it to a pure interpolation-only i2v path. It may compress ordinary setup or a necessary tool/view change, but cannot hide SKU, material-state, water-mark, offcut, or identity discontinuity.
- First-last-frame mode always describes only the physically reachable change between supplied frames. Never use either pure-i2v profile to add an extra event or contradict the visual references. `editorial_bridge` is an edited two-beat delivery mode, not first-last-frame interpolation.
- Do not impose a minimum word count. A simple i2v transition should stay short once action, consequence, camera role, endpoint, and continuity lock are present.

PLAN QUALITY GATES

1. IDENTITY PACKET: identity_truth is mandatory. Select sources by visual identity, not folder name. small_label also requires exact_print_truth.
2. ONE ORDINARY TASK: one person, one coherent scene, one small need, one continuous operation, and one return to life.
3. ROUTE PHYSICS: route and form_factor agree. Every action_family is allowed by that route. The fifth segment uses a route-specific return action.
   Installation-sensitive actions such as press_frame, hang, squeegee/trim/press_edge, press_seal, and secure require matching installation/action evidence; route_default cannot invent hardware or machine structure.
4. EXACT STATES: state_id values are unique; segment from_state_id/to_state_id match anchors; action.end_state equals the visible destination state.
5. REACHABILITY: visible_change contains one main action and one direct physical consequence. If start and end states need multiple hidden installation steps, repair the later anchor before generation.
6. CONTACT: installation operations name contact, path, speed, resistance, and endpoint. Verification and return segments do not invent product contact or friction when the shot does not show it. Flexible products may legally change form while identity and material behavior remain locked.
7. EVIDENCE: product_fact/result information uses fact_ids. Functional claims cite only result evidence that explicitly allows functional claims.
8. NATURALISM: one ordinary place, one main light, 35-50mm equivalent view, one actor, two or three purposeful life traces, and no spectacle language.
9. CAMERA: one camera role and one behavior per shot. Locked camera is preferred when movement does not reveal anything. Macro appears at most once. Pure-i2v modes require `transition=none`; `editorial_bridge` requires exactly one occlusion/match_action cut inside the same established task.
10. COPY: zero to three lines. Prepare/apply default to 无. A non-empty line has a copy_role, copy_basis, copy_reference, and target-anchor copy_visual_evidence. Generic slogans and action-tutorial captions fail.
11. EARNED RETURN: anchor 06 resumes the opening activity in the same space. A pedestal, packshot wall, or unrelated final poster fails.

ADJACENT-ANCHOR REACHABILITY AND CAMERA ROLES

- Write visible_change before writing a prompt. If it needs “and then” more than once, split/rebuild the endpoint.
- Use `hold_context` for ordinary need, `follow_contact` for tool/material contact, `reveal_result` for a complete verification area, `bridge_space` for one existing doorway, and `return_to_master` to recover the opening axis.
- Default to locked. Use short_track only to follow a contact path, small_push_in only to make a result readable, and short_follow only to cross an already visible passage.
- `transition=none` is mandatory for `motion_only_i2v` and `chronological_shot`. `editorial_bridge` requires exactly one `occlusion` or `match_action`; it can compress ordinary setup/tool handling or a necessary verify/return viewpoint change, but cannot hide product-state, water-mark, offcut, tool, or identity discontinuity.
- Camera movement and complex subject movement cannot both dominate. When the hand/tool is the action, keep the room stable. When crossing a doorway is the action, do not add product handling.

FORM-FACTOR IDENTITY AND PHYSICS LOCKS

Choose one block and insert it positively into every anchor/video prompt:

- rigid: stable silhouette, proportions, part count, seams, controls, labels, orientation, scale, and grounded contact.
- textile_panel: stable color, weave cues, print scale, edge treatment, header/track and width; shape may fold and drape under gravity according to the planned state.
- tensioned_mesh: stable mesh density, color, edge tape/frame, zipper/magnet layout and installed span; loose material may flex, installed material remains tensioned between verified attachment points.
- roll_to_sheet: stable material, width, print/opacity, roll core and feed direction; the remaining roll becomes smaller only as the visible sheet length increases; adhered regions are coplanar with the surface.
- decal_transfer: stable exact artwork, text, border, color, scale, cut line and orientation; backing and decal separate at one peel boundary; adhered regions stay flush to the target surface.
- consumable_film: stable roll width, core, repeated print interval, feed direction and film thickness cues; the web remains continuous through the machine and only the verified cup-rim area is sealed/cut.

SCENE-PREVIEW GATE FOR 01

Reject anchor 01 before continuing when any item fails:

- It looks like a studio, concept ad, luxury showroom, or empty CGI room rather than a plausible lived-in place.
- Product scale, window/cup/hand relationship, actor role, or installation surface is unclear.
- More than three decorative background objects compete with the task.
- Light comes from several dramatic directions or creates visible haze/beams.
- The opening need cannot naturally lead to the selected route's first action.

TARGETED ANCHOR RETRY

Regenerate only anchor {ANCHOR_ID}. The prior candidate failed for one visible reason: {ONE_SPECIFIC_DEFECT}. Preserve product truth, exact print, route, form factor, approved endpoint, world plate, scene landmarks, screen axis, actor identity, key light, and every item not named in the defect. Correct only that defect and return one full 9:16 endpoint image.

Name visible corrections: wrong SKU/color/artwork, illegal rigid film, fabric defying gravity, missing mesh tension, broken roll continuity, wrong peel boundary, cup-rim misalignment, hand losing contact, room drift, wrong endpoint, duplicate product, or fake spectacle.

SINGLE-FRAME QC ORDER

1. SKU: same color, artwork/text, edges, hardware, accessories, and scale.
2. PHYSICS: legal material shape, gravity/tension/roll/peel/feed behavior, and verified installation method.
3. CONTACT: hands, surface, frame, cup rim, machine, and shadows meet correctly.
4. ENDPOINT: current anchor state and one information change are visible without explanation.
5. WORLD: same actor, room, landmarks, axis, and light.
6. NATURALISM: ordinary location and behavior; no studio spectacle, magic proof, or hero-poster composition.

SEQUENCE QC

Review 01-06 in order. Fail when an action restarts, reverses, teleports, changes SKU, changes artwork/text, violates form-factor physics, loses contact, changes room/light without cause, invents a result, or ends outside the original daily activity. Repair the earliest later anchor that causes the break and re-check forward.

FIRST-LAST-FRAME VIDEO PROMPT TEMPLATE

`motion_only_i2v` Chinese (default when frames are strong):

动作从首帧立即开始：{SUBJECT}只完成一次{ACTION_FAMILY}——{EXACT_MOTION}，沿{PATH_AND_DIRECTION}移动并在{CONTACT}保持真实接触；动作直接造成{PHYSICAL_CONSEQUENCE}。镜头{ONE_CAMERA_BEHAVIOR}，只为{CAMERA_ROLE_AND_MOTIVATION}。保持真实速度，末端停稳，精确落到尾帧的“{EXACT_END_STATE}”。保持同一 SKU、尺度、人物、房间地标和主光连续。

`chronological_shot` Chinese (Wan/LTX when fuller context is required):

{SUBJECT}从首帧姿态开始，以{SPEED}完成一次{ACTION_FAMILY}：{EXACT_MOTION}，沿{PATH_AND_DIRECTION}移动，在{CONTACT}保持接触并呈现{RESISTANCE_OR_TENSION}；经过后只出现{PHYSICAL_CONSEQUENCE}。镜头{ONE_CAMERA_BEHAVIOR}，岗位是{CAMERA_ROLE}，目的是{CAMERA_MOTIVATION}。动作末端自然减速并停到尾帧的“{EXACT_END_STATE}”。{SELECTED_FORM_FACTOR_LOCK}；同一 SKU、尺度、人物、场景轴线和光线保持连续。

`editorial_bridge` Chinese (multi-shot engine or NLE only; never pure interpolation i2v):

第一拍从首帧立即承接：{BEAT_ONE_ACTION_AND_VISIBLE_CONSEQUENCE}。当{EXISTING_OCCLUDER_OR_MATCHED_ACTION}占满或对齐画面时，只做一次{OCCLUSION_OR_MATCH_ACTION}切镜；第二拍仍在同一任务与同一空间中，{BEAT_TWO_ACTION}，随后停到尾帧的“{EXACT_END_STATE}”。两拍保持同一 SKU、人物、工具清单、材料状态演进、场景地标和主光；切镜不隐藏水痕、余料、商品状态或身份变化。成片按两镜头剪辑，不作为纯首尾帧插值提交。

English motion-only variant:

Starting immediately from the supplied first frame, {SUBJECT} performs one {ACTION_FAMILY}: {EXACT_MOTION} along {PATH_AND_DIRECTION}, maintaining contact at {CONTACT}; the direct visible consequence is {PHYSICAL_CONSEQUENCE}. The camera uses {ONE_CAMERA_BEHAVIOR} only to {CAMERA_ROLE_AND_MOTIVATION}. Keep a natural real-time pace, settle, and land exactly on the supplied end-frame state: {EXACT_END_STATE}. Preserve the same SKU, scale, actor, room landmarks, and key light.

Do not append a long negative list. A direct prompt may end with “无字幕、无水印” when needed. Only an approved `editorial_bridge` may name its single `occlusion` or `match_action`; the two pure-i2v profiles do not add cuts, montage, time codes, or a second event.

FINAL-FILM COPY

Copy is optional. Use zero to three lines across five segments. Give a line one job: `need`, `condition`, `proof`, or `closure`. Prepare/apply segments default to 无; the hands already explain cleaning, aligning, smoothing, trimming, peeling, or sealing.

Build each kept line from:

target-anchor visible evidence -> safe everyday meaning/necessary condition -> shortest spoken line

Before keeping a line, point to the exact target anchor and answer “what visible object or result authorizes every word?” If the answer relies on instructions, category assumptions, or a different frame, delete or rewrite it. Functional conditions belong in the sentence.

Privacy-film example: `临街坐着，难免在意路过的人。` (need, anchor 01), `白天外面更亮时，窗面先映出街景。` (condition/proof, exterior verification), `回到里面，窗外还是看得见。` (closure, interior resumed state). Do not use `膜要先留足裁边余量` or `再沿玻璃边缘裁齐` as finished-film copy.

Reject generic lines containing: 重新定义品质, 品质之选, 极致体验, 非凡之选, 开启美好生活, 不止于此, 匠心品质, 高端大气, 细节自会说话, 留下这一眼, 轻松搞定, 不止好看, 一步到位, 质感拉满.
